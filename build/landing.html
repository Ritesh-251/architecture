Features

Everything you need to design confidently

From first sketch to final render, Architecture Master covers the full design workflow.

📐

2D Precision Canvas

Draw walls, rooms, and corridors with snap-to-grid accuracy and real unit measurements.

🧊

Live 3D Preview

Instantly switch to a real-time 3D view powered by Three.js WebGL. Orbit, pan, and zoom freely.

🛋️

200+ Element Library

Drag-and-drop furniture, doors, windows, kitchen, and bathroom elements from a rich library.

🏢

Multi-Floor Support

Add floors with a single click. Each floor has its own independent canvas layer.

☁️

Save & Sync

Designs save automatically to your account. Access your work from any browser, any time.

📤

Export Anywhere

Download your design as a PDF floorplan, PNG image, or a GLTF 3D model file.
